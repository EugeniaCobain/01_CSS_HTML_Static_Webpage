About the site

It is a common belief that cemeteries are eerie, creepy places, but in many countries they are seen as big quiet parks where people go walking or jogging, meet their friends, have a picnics, take naps, read and walk their dogs. Some of them are even in the middle of the city, with no walls or fences surrounfing them. In addition to that, they are not only quien but also open air museums.
